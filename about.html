Centre for Marine and Offshore Technology Development

Introduction

The Centre for Marine and Offshore Technology Development was commissioned on Thursday, 1st June, 2017 and academic activities started in April, 2018 with the first set of ten (10) students. The Centre was set up by Shell Petroleum Development Company as a new initiative in its University Assistance Programme, to strengthen the intervention strategies from ‘within’ the university environment. Domiciling the Centre for Marine and Offshore Technology Development within the university environment will also serve as a rallying point for research and development, and sponsored discipline experts (sabbaticals, Professorial Chairs, etc) of the company and the Joint Venture partners which include The Nigerian National Petroleum Company (NNPC), Total Exploration and Production Nigeria (TEPNG) and the Nigerian Agip Oil Company (NAOC). It is expected that this effort will within a short time attract support of other stake holders and broaden the collaboration to include players in offshore oil and gas sector, and maritime companies operating in Nigeria, as well as those providing consultancy and other specialized services across the spectrum of the maritime industry. The Centre is governed by a Trust Deed and Governance Framework between the Industry (SPDC) and the Rivers State University.

Who We Are

Advancing Engineering Expertise for the Offshore Industry

The center of excellence in Marine & Offshore Engineering, established within the prestigious Rivers State University in Port Harcourt Nigeria, is dedicated to the development of highly skilled postgraduate engineers. Since its inception in 2018, the Center has rapidly evolved to become one of Nigeria's premiere technical and engineering institutions, specifically catering to the offshore oil & gas industry. Our center is a beacon of excellence, renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities, comprehensive training program, and a team of highly qualified professionals.

Empowering Offshore Excellence

The center of excellence in Marine & Offshore Engineering is committed to providing a base with the needed up-to-date technology, requisite knowledge and skilled resource groups that will be available to carry out studies and research effectively. Offering a forum where university and industry professionals can collaborate and interact to share knowledge and develop skills and also training Marine and Offshore Engineering graduates that will have the skills and competences required for effective performance at the workplace at the end of their training

Driven by Purpose & Principle

At the heart of our mission are core values that define our approach to education, research, and professional engagement. We uphold excellence in all our endeavors, setting high standards across academic and technical pursuits. We foster a culture of innovation, encouraging creative thinking and the advancement of new technologies. With integrity as a cornerstone, we remain committed to ethical conduct, transparency, and accountability. Above all, we champion sustainability, embracing practices that protect the environment and support long-term development.

Engr. Dr. Eferebo I. Sylvanus is a seasoned marine engineer and naval architect with over 20 years of experience in engineering, marine operations, and ship design. He holds a B.Eng. in Marine Engineering, an M.Tech. in Naval Architecture & Ship Construction, and a PhD in Naval Architecture from Rivers State University. He is a registered engineer with COREN, a member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), the Nigerian Institution of Marine Engineers and Naval Architects (NIMENA), and a Fellow of the Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC). He currently chairs COREN’s Engineering Regulation, Monitoring, and Enforcement (ERME) Committee for the Marine & Offshore Division. Dr. Sylvanus is the founder of Somatrix Marine Ltd, with expertise in marine consulting, ship design, construction management, and surveys, operating across Nigeria, South Africa, and beyond. He has served as a Principal Technical Consultant to the National Assembly on Nigerian Content Development, highlighting his strong influence on policy and industry growth. He is recognized for his leadership in advancing local content in Nigeria’s blue economy, his contributions to engineering education, and his extensive research publications in reputable international journals.
