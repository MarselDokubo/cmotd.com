Who We Are

Advancing Engineering Expertise for the Offshore Industry

The Center for Marine and Offshore Technology Development (CMOTD), established within the prestigious Rivers State University in Port Harcourt Nigeria, is dedicated to the development of highly skilled postgraduate engineers. Since it's inception in 2021, the Center has rapidly evolved to become one of Nigeria's premiere technical and engineering institutions, specifically catering to the offshore oil & gas industry. Our center is a beacon of excellence, renowned for it's state-of-the-art facilities, comprehensive training program, and a team of highly qualified professionals.

Empowering Offshore Excellence

The Center for Marine and Offshore Technology Development (CMOTD) is committed to providing a base with the needed up-to-date technology, requisite knowledge and skilled resource groups that will be available to carry out studies and research effectively. Offering a forum where university and industry professionals can collaborate and interact to share knowledge and develop skills and also training Marine and Offshore Engineering graduates that will have the skills and competences required for effective performance at the workplace at the end of their training

Driven by Purpose & Principle

At the heart of our mission are core values that define our approach to education, research, and professional engagement. We uphold excellence in all our endeavors, setting high standards across academic and technical pursuits. We foster a culture of innovation, encouraging creative thinking and the advancement of new technologies. With integrity as a cornerstone, we remain committed to ethical conduct, transparency, and accountability. Through collaboration, we build strong partnerships with industry, academia, and government to drive progress. Above all, we champion sustainability, embracing practices that protect the environment and support long-term development.
