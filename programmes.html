Our Courses

INTERNATIONAL SHIP AND PORT FACILITY SECURITY (ISPS) CODE

The ISPS Code training course aims to equip maritime security personnel with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills.

HYSYS Software Training

HYSYS is a powerful process simulation software used extensively in the chemical, oil and gas, and energy industries developed by AspenTech.

Pressure Vessels & Heat Exchanges Design

PV Elite is a comprehensive package used for the design, analysis, and evaluation of pressure vessels and heat exchangers.

Process Pipeline Design & Analysis (Statics & Dynamics)-Ceaser II

Ceaser II is a comprehensive pipe stress analysis software widely used in the engineering and construction industries.

Plant Design Management System (PDMS)

PDMS is a highly sophisticated software suite developed by AVEVA for designing and managing plant and marine projects.

Integrated Safety Management

The ISM training program aims to equip participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to implement and manage integrated safety management systems effectively.

Lead Marine Author

The Lead Marine Auditors is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills needed for effective marine auditing.

Marine Coating Technology & Paint Production Training

This training addresses the chemistry, production process, selection and application techniques, and performance evaluation of marine coatings.

Rigging & Scaffolding Training

Rigging and Scaffolding involves covering essential safety practices, technical knowledge, and practical skills necessary for safe and efficient rigging and scaffolding operations.

Solar Engineering Development & Installation Training

Solar Engineering Development & Installation is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills needed for professionas in the solar energy industry.

Professional Project Management Training

Project Management is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills needed for effective project management.

Flow Metering & Instrument Calibration Training

Flow Metering and Instrument Callibration covers the fundamental principles, technical details, and practical skills necessary for accurate measurement and callibration of flow meters and other instruments.

Automation, Instrumentation & Control Operation

Class Welding, Fabrication & Qualification Training.
